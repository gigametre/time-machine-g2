Setup tips
  1. Place the phone on a tripod, directly beside the finish line, looking across the line.
  2. Drag the orange vertical line so it sits exactly on the real finish line.
  3. Use good lighting and keep spectators out of the finish-line strip.
  4. Press Arm detection just before the runners arrive.
  5. This prototype is for crossing order / review, not official fully automatic timing.
